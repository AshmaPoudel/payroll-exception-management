01 Problem Statement
No SLA ownership

Issues sit unresolved for days with no timer or accountability — nobody knows when a ticket is overdue.

Duplicate ticket submissions

Employees re-submit unresolved issues, creating ghost tickets that consume analyst time and inflate queue size.

Delayed escalation

Escalation depends on who you know. Critical salary issues can take 3–5 days to reach the right resolver.

No visibility dashboard

Ops managers must chase individual analysts for status — no aggregate view of open tickets or breach risk.

Manual tracking in spreadsheets

Resolution data siloed in individual spreadsheets — reporting inaccurate, root-cause analysis impossible.
